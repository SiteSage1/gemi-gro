About Me

Crafting immersive digital worlds with motion, code, and emotion since 2018

Hi, I’m Alex Rivera — a multidisciplinary designer and developer obsessed with the intersection of motion graphics, interactive web experiences, and cinematic storytelling.

From award-winning brand animations for global campaigns to immersive 3D web installations, I believe every project should evoke wonder. Currently based in Lisbon, Portugal, I collaborate with visionary studios and independent creators across Europe and the US.

When I’m not designing, you’ll find me experimenting with generative art, shooting 35mm film, or chasing sunsets with my drone.
